User Manual

Welcome to the G1 Compiler User Manual. This section provides guidance on how to use the G1 Compiler effectively.

System Overview

This system utilizes algorithms to transform regular expressions into various equivalent forms, such as DFAs, CFGs, and PDAs. These transformations allow for easier understanding and analysis of regular expressions within the context of formal language theory.

Getting Started

To begin using the G1 Compiler, follow these steps:

1: While in the homepage, click on COMPILER in order to go to the Compiler Window

2: Once you are in the Compiler Window, choose a regular expression from the dropdown menu

3: After choosing a regular expression, enter a string to check its validity

4: Look on the DFA below, and follow through the tracing

5: Afterwards, click the CFG or PDA located on the top-right corner

6: Once redirected to the CFG or PDA window, here you can scroll to look at the cfg or pda of the respective regular expression
